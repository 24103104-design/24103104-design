The Main Content Area

This is the main section of the homepage. According to the requirements, the **main** tag has a light grey background and a slightly larger font size. The **p** tag has a line height of 1.8 for improved readability.

All pages are linked through the navigation bar above. Click on "About Us" or "Contact" to navigate to the other two files. The header and navigation bar remain consistent across all pages.

CSS Styling Review

The styles in **styles.css** apply to: **body** (font & size), **header** (background), **nav** (text color), **main** (background & size), **footer** (text color), **li** (display & width), **h1** (alignment, font-family, color), and **p** (line height).
